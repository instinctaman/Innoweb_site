Job Description

We are seeking a results-driven and creative Digital Marketing Executive to plan, implement, and manage online marketing campaigns that promote our brand, products, and services. The ideal candidate should have strong knowledge of current digital marketing tools, strategies, and best practices, with the ability to lead integrated digital campaigns from concept to execution.

Key Responsibilities

  • Plan and execute SEO/SEM strategies to increase organic visibility and search engine rankings.
  • Manage content creation and distribution across websites, blogs, and social media platforms.
  • Optimize marketing campaigns to improve ROI and reduce costs.
  • Execute email marketing campaigns and manage marketing automation workflows.
  • Collaborate with designers, developers, and content creators to deliver engaging campaigns.
  • Stay up to date with the latest digital marketing trends, technologies, and best practices.

Requirements & Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Business, Communications, or related field.
  • Proven experience (1–3 years) in digital marketing or related role.
  • Strong understanding of SEO, SEM, PPC, social media marketing, and content marketing.
  • Hands-on experience with tools like Google Ads, Facebook Ads Manager, Google Analytics, WordPress, and email marketing platforms.
  • Knowledge of graphic design tools (Canva, Photoshop) is a plus.
  • Excellent communication, analytical, and project management skills.

Key Skills

  • Digital Marketing Strategy
  • SEO & SEM
  • Social Media Management
  • Google Ads / Meta Ads
  • Email Marketing & Automation
  • Data Analytics & Reporting
